# -v.2-Portfolio
A cinematic, dark-themed portfolio homepage featuring a deep navy-to-amber gradient, elegant serif typography, and a glowing horizon effect. Designed with an immersive, minimal aesthetic and a strong visual focus on identity and craft.

// Background // 
-- Fully animaetd sunset scene

- Gradient Sky (deep violet-black at the top bleeding through wine red and burnt sienna down to a bright orange-gold horizon band, then darkening back to near-black at the bottom)
- Sun disc — sits right on the horizon, glowing white-gold core with a radial bloom that bleeds orange-red across the whole lower sky
- Crepuscular rays — 8 volumetric light rays fanning out from the sun, gently swaying on a slow sine wave
- Drifting clouds — slow horizontal ellipses lit in orange and deep red, catching the sun's light
- Water waves — below the horizon in crimson, burnt orange and dark red tones, replacing the blue ocean
- Sun reflection — a wobbly column of gold-orange light shimmering down the water surface from the sun
- Ember particles — warm orange and yellow firefly-like motes drifting upward instead of the cold blue bubbles
- Stars — faint twinkling dots only in the upper 30% of the sky where it's dark enough to see them
